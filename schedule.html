💡 Tips From Real Parents

🔁

Consistency Is Everything

The schedule only works if you stick to it — even on weekends and holidays. Your baby's internal clock will thank you.

🛁

The Bedtime Routine Is the Secret

Bath → books → lotion → PJs → bottle → bed. In that order. Every night. It signals to your baby that sleep is coming.

🤸

Start Tummy Time Early

Even 2 minutes on your chest counts. It builds the neck and core strength babies need — and gets them used to the position.

⏰

Wake Times Matter Too

A consistent morning wake time is just as important as bedtime. If they sleep in, the whole schedule shifts. Hold the wake time.

🌙

7PM Bedtime Is the Goal

It sounds early, but an earlier bedtime actually leads to better sleep — less overtired, less fussiness, longer stretches.

💪

It Takes a Few Weeks

Don't give up after 3 days. Babies need 2–3 weeks of consistency to start adapting. Push through the hard stretch — it's worth it.
